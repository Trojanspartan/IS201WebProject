Golf, practice, and steady improvement

One of my interests is learning golf. I like it because progress is measurable, the skill ceiling is high, and small technical changes can make a big difference over time.

Practice Plan Video Tableau Graph Open the mini web app

How I would structure a week of practice

  1. Short game focus
    • Lag putting from 20 to 40 feet
    • Three-foot pressure putts
    • Simple chipping contact drills
  2. Ball striking focus
    • Half-swing wedge work
    • Alignment stick drill for start line
    • Impact location check with foot spray
  3. On-course decision making
    • Play for the widest part of the fairway
    • Aim away from the biggest miss
    • Choose targets before every shot
