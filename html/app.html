Golf Practice Plan Builder

Build a practice session that matches where you plan to practice, what you want to work on, and how much time you have. The app creates a detailed timeline with exact minute totals, specific drills, and clear goals like bucket counts, rep targets, make percentages, or up-and-down scores.

Build your session

Tip: if you choose both areas, the session usually starts with longer swings first and finishes in the short-game area, so you end with touch, scoring, and pressure.

Your practice plan

Select your practice area, choose an optional focus, set your time, and click Generate practice plan.

How this app builds the plan

  • It adjusts the session based on whether you choose the range, the short-game area, or both.
  • It emphasizes your selected focus when one makes sense, but still keeps the session useful and balanced.
  • It assigns exact minutes so the session adds up to your selected total.
  • Each drill now includes a suggested rep target and a measurable success goal, so the plan feels more like a coach-led session.
